Two distinct projects that share a design philosophy: learning that responds to the learner. For Vyond, designed the comprehensive global certification program for their web-based video production tool — establishing proficiency benchmarks adopted by their worldwide user base. For Frontdoor, architected personalized compliance training using complex branching scenarios with real-time performance-based adaptations.

## Vyond Global Certification

The challenge was defining what "proficient" looks like for a creative tool used by tens of thousands of people across wildly different use cases — L&D professionals, marketers, HR teams, educators. Proficiency isn't the same across those audiences.

Designed a certification framework that assessed core competencies shared across all users while allowing for role-based pathways to more specialized skills. Assessments were performance-based — learners demonstrated proficiency by doing, not by answering multiple choice questions about doing.

The certification was adopted as the official platform proficiency standard for Vyond's global user base.

## Frontdoor Adaptive Compliance

Compliance training for a distributed field workforce presents a different kind of design problem. Learners arrive with different baseline knowledge, different job functions, and different regulatory exposure. A one-size-fits-all module wastes time for experienced employees and under-serves newer ones.

Architected a branching system that adapted in real time based on performance — not just "you got this wrong, try again," but genuine pathway divergence based on demonstrated understanding. Learners who demonstrated mastery early moved faster. Those who struggled received targeted remediation before proceeding.

## Shared Outcomes

- Vyond global certification: adopted as the platform proficiency standard
- Frontdoor: branching logic with real-time performance-based adaptation
- Both projects built to ADDIE framework with deep SME collaboration throughout
