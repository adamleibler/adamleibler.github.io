The Narrative

Built on Curiosity. Sharpened by Mastery.

Principal Instructional Designer  │  AI-Enabled eLearning Developer  │  U.S. Army Veteran

Twenty-five years in learning and performance, built on a simple bias: find the actual gap first. At Twitch, that meant building engineering education from scratch for 700 globally distributed engineers and getting new-hire ramp to under 30 days. At Terminix, it was a Workday Learn transformation serving 10,000+ employees. More recently: federal agencies and PE-backed enterprises, AI-enabled capability transformation and org diagnostics.

Outside work: the hobbies are many, the cars are loud, and the novel is coming. Certified car audio installer and part-time JDM rebuilder. Fourteen instruments, including first-chair cello in the Georgia All-State Orchestra. Three plays produced in high school, one staged at a professional theater. Committed sci-fi and fantasy fan — which probably explains the novel.

The Human Element

Meet Ripley.

Service Dog in Training  │  Chief Morale Officer  │  Conflicts of Interest: Treat-Based

Ripley is my service dog in training, full-time Chief Morale Officer, and copawlot on Late Night Space Flight — my Twitch show where I fly Elite: Dangerous to real astronomical objects at accurate galactic coordinates and talk astrophysics in real time. Her production credits are mostly naps. She works for treats.

She's also the third in a line: Noble, then Leo, now Ripley. For a lot of veterans, a service dog is real infrastructure — mobility support, and a steady anchor through PTSD, anxiety, and depression. Three dogs in, I've seen exactly how much that work matters.

So I back the organizations that train and place service dogs with veterans at no cost, and I'm putting more behind it — volunteering and fundraising, not just applause. If that's your world too, I'd like to hear from you.
