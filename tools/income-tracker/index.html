Veteran resources
Vocational Rehabilitation & Employment (VR&E)
Chapter 31 benefits — can fund course creation as a self-employment track. Call 1-800-827-1000 and ask specifically for "Chapter 31."
Hire Heroes USA
Free job search support, resume review, and interview coaching for veterans and military spouses.
hireheroesusa.org →
VetJobs
Job board focused on veteran-friendly employers. Good for W2 roles with companies that actively recruit vets.
vetjobs.com →
DAV / American Legion Emergency Grants
Emergency financial assistance for disabled veterans. Contact local chapter for fastest response.
